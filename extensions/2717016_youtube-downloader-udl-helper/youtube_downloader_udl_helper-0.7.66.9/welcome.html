UDL Helper has been installed

We are required to get your permission to collect analytics. The following technical data is needed to maintain and develop UDL Helper:

  • Event source (browser family and version, identifiers of an online video, method of media extraction)
  • Health reports (indication of a successful operation: initialization, format parsing, recompression etc.)
  • Crash reports (media details needed to reproduce the extension failure)

This information is aggregated (i.e. no single user is being tracked) and used for the following purposes:

  • to find out which videos/formats/services UDL Helper is unable to handle
  • to estimate an audience size by browser/extension build/etc.
  • to schedule fixes and updates for UDL Helper

For a user, enabling analytics is optional and won't affect the rest of UDL Helper functionality. It can also be changed later in the Settings menu available from UDL Helper button.

Please click the relevant button to make your choice and accept the privacy policy and the license agreement.
