Welcome!

Instapaper Firefox Add-On is the best way to save articles to Instapaper on Firefox.

Information We Collect

In order to save articles to Instapaper, we collect certain information as part of this Firefox Add-On:

  • URLs of articles you save.
  • HTML of articles you save.
  • Selected text on the page when you save articles (this becomes the article description in Instapaper).

Instapaper only collects information when you save an article or take an action.

Your Consent

Before continuing, we need your consent to collect data in the manner described above.

Without your consent, the Instapaper Firefox Add-On would not function properly. In that case, we recommend uninstalling the Add-On.
