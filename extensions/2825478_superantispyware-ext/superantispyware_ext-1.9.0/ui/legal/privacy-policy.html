Privacy Policy

Version: 4.1
Last update: 28th June 2024

1. SUPERAntiSpyware Ext does not collect, store or share any of your Personal Data

1.1 SUPERAntiSpyware Ext and our search partner are designed so that we do not collect, store or share any of your Personal Data.

2. What is Personal Data?

2.1 Any data that can be used to identify you, or behaviour and preferences that can be traced back to you, is Personal Data.

2.2 Our definition of Personal Data is based on international standards in privacy laws and regulation of the European Union, including the General Data Protection Regulation.

3. What data is transmitted to SUPERAntiSpyware Ext's or our search partner's servers?

3.1 All data transmitted to our or our search partner's servers is necessary to maintain the core functionality of SUPERAntiSpyware Ext, or transmitted for statistical purposes. The data transmitted includes:

3.1.1 Your search queries are transmitted to our search partner. Your search queries are not collected or stored by SUPERAntiSpyware Ext or our search partner.

3.1.2 URLs of websites you visit are transmitted to our servers as part of SUPERAntiSpyware Ext's protection against malware, phising and other malicious websites. The URLs of websites you visit are not collected or stored by SUPERAntiSpyware Ext in any form.

3.1.3 SUPERAntiSpyware Ext transmits statistical data to our servers for the purpose of further improvement of SUPERAntiSpyware Ext. All statistical data is collected in aggregated and anonymised form, includes no personal data, and cannot by traced back to you..

3.1.4 Statistical data is transmitted when:

  • The extension is enabled
  • The extension is uninstalled
  • The extension used for the first time in one day
  • Certain thresholds of the number of searches made with our search partner are reached

3.1.5 The statistical data transmitted at various points includes:

  • SUPERAntiSpyware Ext version
  • Browser version
  • Operating system
  • User country
  • The threshold of the number of searches made with our search partner reached
  • Time of installation of the SUPERAntiSpyware Ext

3.2 Statistical data is only transmitted if you agree to this privacy policy by enabling the extension after installation.

3.2.1 You may opt-out of statistical data transmission at any time in the extension settings.

4. What data is collected or stored by SUPERAntiSpyware?

4.1 No personal data is collected or stored by SUPERAntiSpyware Ext or our search partner.

4.2 No data about the content of your searches or visited websites is collected or stored in any form by SUPERAntiSpyware Ext or our search partner.

4.3 For statistical purposes, SUPERAntiSpyware Ext collects and stores aggregated anonymised data as specified in paragraph 3.1.5.

4.4 No statistical data collected or stored by SUPERAntiSpyware Ext can be traced back to you.

5. Your search queries are anonymous

5.1 When you search with SUPERAntiSpyware Ext, only your anonymised search query is sent to our search partner. We don’t send any information or data that can be used to identify you, profile you, or connect you to a search query or any other online activity. Neither do we keep a record or history of your search queries. Your search is completely anonymous.

6. What happens when you leave SUPERAntiSpyware Ext, by clicking a link in the search results page?

6.1 When you click on a search result or sponsored link, you leave our site.

6.2 For continued privacy protection outside of our site, click on the ‘Anonymous Mode’ icon (insert icon) next to each search result and sponsored link to browse anonymously.

7. What if you use a different search engine with SUPERAntiSpyware Ext?

7.1 We recommend you use the StartPage search engine, as it provides you with significantly greater privacy protection than using other search engines. If you use an alternative search engine, they may not respect your privacy, and your search behaviour may not remain private.

8. Information requests from authorities

8.1 We will only comply with information requests from authorities where we are legally required to do so. We do not actively assist authorities in any information gathering.
