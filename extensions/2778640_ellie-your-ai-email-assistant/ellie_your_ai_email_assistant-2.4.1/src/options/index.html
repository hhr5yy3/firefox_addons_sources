Email training

Your assistant will be able to generate replies without any training, however by providing it with past emails that you have sent will improve it drastically.

Ellie will use these emails to mimic your tone and also it will remember the information in the emails for writing future replies.

Email training is currently only available for customers on one of our paid plans.

Instructions

  • The easiest way to provide Ellie with your email data is forward emails in your sent folder to us as attachments.

  • You can forward us 10 sent emails to train Ellie with (to send more and improve the training you can upgrade your plan).

  • You can forward us 50 sent emails at a time up to a max of 200, the more you send us the better the training will be!

  • Your email data will be stored in the AI's memory banks and used as a reference when creating new replies. Your data is private and will not be shared with any other users.
