We are required to get your permission to collect analytics. The following information is needed to maintain and develop Screenshot YouTube Video:

  • browsing activity data (URLs of online video players where Screenshot YouTube Video is active, installation IDs - large random numbers)
  • technical data (event type, browser family and version, extension version)

This information is aggregated (i.e. no single user is being tracked) and is used to estimate an audience size and to find cases where Screenshot YouTube Video is failing.

This function is optional and won't affect the rest of Screenshot YouTube Video.
