Tab Sidebar Options

How to Fold Tabs

Tabs between marker tabs in the sidebar are foldable.
You can create a marker tab with "New Marker Tab" in the right click menu.
A tab with URL "data:text/plain,tab-siderbar-marker:MarkerNameHere" becomes a marker tab.
