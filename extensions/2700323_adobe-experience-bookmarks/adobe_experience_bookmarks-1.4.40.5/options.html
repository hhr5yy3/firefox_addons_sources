Welcome to Adobe Experience Cloud Bookmarks configuration!

Add an Experience Cloud organization to activate the extension. You can add as many organizations as you want. Once an organization is added, click on the extension icon to open the bookmarks.

Organizations:

Add new organization

Enable the "Automatically Import Organizations" setting to let the extension fill the list of organizations for you.

If adding organizations manually looks intricate, just log in to Experience Cloud and click on the extension icon. The organization(s) will be imported automatically.
