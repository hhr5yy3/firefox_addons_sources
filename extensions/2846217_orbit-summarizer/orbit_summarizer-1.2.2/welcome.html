Welcome to Orbit by Mozilla

Thank you for installing our extension. Orbit is designed to enhance your browsing experience by generating summaries and providing you with quick answers to queries about the content of pages you visit.

Essential Data Collection

Orbit needs access to the content of the web pages you visit in order to generate summaries and provide real-time answers. This data is processed immediately and never stored. This is essential for the extension's core functionality and cannot be disabled.

For more details, please review Orbit's Privacy Notice.

Optional Data Collection

To further enhance your experience and ensure optimal performance, you can choose to allow Orbit to collect additional data for error monitoring and performance tracking. This data helps us identify and resolve issues quickly, improving stability and compatibility across different environments. The optional data we collect includes:

  • Error details, quality of service information, and the URL where issues occur, so we can track and fix errors effectively.
  • Browser and operating system information to ensure compatibility and improve overall performance.

By opting in, you help us improve Orbit's performance without impacting the speed or reliability of your browsing experience.
