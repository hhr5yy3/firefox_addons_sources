SIMTEST.it Mobile proxy is one of the features of SIMTEST.it service that enables you to browse 3G and 4G mobile networks in more than 70 countries worldwide.

If you want to know how your mobile website is being displayed to users worldwide, SIMTEST.it Mobile proxy is all you need. Browse 3G or 4G mobile internet as if you were physically in the target country, check website layout and behavior, or observe your service functionality. You can even combine it with a mobile billing test to ensure that your service gets charged properly every time.
