About
------

Spell-check is based on Serbian Hunspell dictionary authored by Milutin Smiljanic (msmiljanic.gm@gmail.com).

The dictionary covers 3,4 million words and word forms. 

Two otherwise identical dictionaries and affix rules are available for Cyrillic and Latin Alphabet:

sr-Cyrl.dic, sr-Cyrl.aff - Serbian Cyrillic alphabet
sr-Latn.dic, sr-Latn.aff - Serbian Latin alphabet
