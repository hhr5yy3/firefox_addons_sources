Signaler, c'est agir

Thank you for installing and using the Signal Spam extension. This is available for your webmail.

Report an illegitimate email or site:

With this extension, you can report an email, unsolicited or fraudulent, from one of the compatible webmails (Gmail, Outlook.com, Yahoo! Mail, Orange, Free, SFR, OVH, LaPoste.net), or report a fraudulent website by clicking on the Signal Spam button installed in your browser toolbar.

To report an email: Open an email or select the emails you want to report and then click the Signal Spam button in your browser toolbar.

To report a fraudulent web page (phishing): Open the web page in your browser, then click on the Signal Spam button in your browser toolbar.

Your reports will contribute to protect other users.

Protection against fraudulent sites:

The Signal Spam extension will alert you, in your webmail, if you open an email containing a link to a fraudulent site. An alert will also be displayed if you visit a website listed as fraudulent.
These alerts only apply to links to websites already listed as fraudulent.
