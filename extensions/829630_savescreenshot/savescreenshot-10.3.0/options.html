%Y - year | %m - month | %d - day | %H - hour | %M - minute | %S - second
%h - host name | %u - URL | %t - page title
Example: %Y%m%d_%H%M%S_%h -> 20181224_123805_www.abc.de
You can enter a directory name here, if you want your screenshots to be placed into a subdirectory of your configured downloads directory. Allowed characters are: A-Z,a-z,0-9,_,-
