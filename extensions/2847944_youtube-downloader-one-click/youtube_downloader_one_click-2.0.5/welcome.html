We are required to get your permission to collect analytics. The following data is needed to maintain and develop YDH:

  • Browsing activity data
    • URLs of online video players
    • Installation IDs (large random numbers)
  • Technical data
    • Browser family and version
    • Method of media extraction (specific to online video service)

This information is aggregated (i.e. no single user is being tracked) and is used for the following purposes:

  • to estimate an audience size by browser/version/etc.
  • to find cases where YDH is failing
  • to schedule updates for YDH

For a user, enabling analytics is optional and won't affect the rest of YDH functionality. It can also be changed later in the Settings menu available from YDH button.

Please make your choice.
